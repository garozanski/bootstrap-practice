Travel Photographer

What is a travel photographer?

A travel photographer is a professional who works in various industries, photographically capturing different geographic locations, cultures and customs. They may work locally or globally, providing photographic services for organizations or individuals1. Travel photography is primarily about photographing places in all their glory. As a travel photographer, you will travel to lots of places and capture them through your camera. The places can be tourist attractions, natural landscapes, hotels and resorts, cultural experiences, and outdoor adventures2.

There are many beautiful places around the world that are perfect for travel photography. Some of the best places to travel for photography include:

  • The Lake District, England
  • Grand Canyon, USA
  • Provence, France
  • Tenerife, Canary Islands
  • Tanzania, Africa
  • Maldives
  • Kefalonia, Greece
  • Kyoto, Japan

These places offer a variety of attractions such as crystal-clear oceans, breathtaking vistas, hiking adventures, safaris and so much more and cater to a variety of interests, budgets and activity levels.

Travel Photography Equipment

If you’re looking to get started with travel photography, there are a few essential pieces of equipment that you’ll need:

  • Main Camera
  • Lenses
  • Action Camera
  • Tripod
  • Camera bag/backpack
  • Lens cleaning kit
